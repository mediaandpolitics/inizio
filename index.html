Does social media reflect the public opinion?

2014 was called the "Super Election" year in Sweden. All over Europe, far right-wing parties have increased in popularity and gained more votes from the people. In Sweden, the fastest growing political party is Sverigedemokraterna. There has also been a rise of interest in important political questions, especially the issue of immigration and refugees.

Social media and the engagement of the immigrant question.

Social media plays a role in the public conversation of important political issues. People today are connecting by sharing and commenting on news articles online. Looking at the top 10 most shared Aftonbladet news articles referencing Sverigedemokraterna, 8 of those articles concerned the immigration and refugee issue.
